Welcome to Green Village

Green Village is an idyllic nature campsite, perfectly situated amidst lush greenery and surrounded by peace and serenity. You will be warmly welcomed in our rural household! Whether you choose the comfort of our modern bungalows or the freedom of a spacious camping pitch, you'll find the perfect base for a relaxing holiday in nature. Escape the daily hustle and bustle and unwind completely and enjoy our homemade traditional food.

Camping in Nature

For true nature lovers, we offer spacious camping pitches, surrounded by greenery. Whether you come with your own tent, caravan, or camper, you'll always find a spot that suits you. Enjoy the peace, fresh air, and beautiful surroundings. Our camping pitches are equipped with the necessary facilities to make your stay as comfortable as possible.
